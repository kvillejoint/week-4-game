Crystal Collector: The Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win by matching your total score to the random number. If your total score goes above the random number you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the random number and crystal values will change as well. Good luck!
